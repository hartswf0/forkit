PHILOSOPHY

THICK DESCRIPTION
Traditional thought experiments strip context. FORKIT restores it. Each scenario includes the smell of the air, the weight of personal history, the sound of footsteps. This is the Cascade Explorer demonstrating thick data navigation.
THE FORK
Y — The primordial distinction. Every scenario presents Track A and Track B. Not abstract options but lived possibilities with names, faces, dialogue. This is the Helix Protocol showing moral DNA.

McLuhan: "The medium is the message." Each experiment offers a different lens for the same ethical content. This is the Tetrad: Enhance / Obsolete / Retrieve / Reverse.

A physical lever creates different moral intuitions than a button. This is The Lever — mobile-first 3D interface where you feel the weight.
